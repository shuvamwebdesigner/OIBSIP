About

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and industrial designer. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s.

Timeline

  • 1955: Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1976: Co-founded Apple Computer, Inc.
  • 1984: Launched the first Macintosh.
  • 1997: Returned to Apple as CEO.
  • 2007: Introduced the iPhone.
  • 2011: Passed away, leaving a lasting legacy.
